Why families choose us

Built on trust.

Safety and transparency aren't features — they're the foundation everything else is built on.

Parent-Approved Accounts
Every teen under 16 signs up with a parent or guardian. We verify age and parental consent before any profile goes live.
Verified Reviews Only
Only verified clients who completed a real booking can leave a review — no anonymous posts, no inflated scores.
Safe In-App Messaging
All communication stays inside TeenWork. No personal phone numbers or emails are shared until both parties confirm.

Work History & References
